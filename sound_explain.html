Hi! If you're here, then you wanted to learn more about why you have to click a pop-up every time you open the metronome.

In order to load our sound files (that nice wood block sound in our metronome), the user must first take an action.

This is to prevent malicious websites from bombarding their users with sounds as soon as they open their website, even before the user has interacted with it.

Unfortunately, in order to accomodate this security feature, we had to create a pop-up to ensure the user interacted with our site before our metronome could play sounds.

Otherwise, if your first action on our site was to click "Play", you may not hear anything because the sound has to finish loading first.

I hope that explanation helps and thank you for your patience.
