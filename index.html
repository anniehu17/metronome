MetronomeBear

Welcome To MetronomeBear!

1. Open Practice Tempo Calculator and type in the goal tempo (refer to Tempo Ranges for approximate BPM for tempo markings).

2. Click on the "Go to Metronome" button to open the metronome.

3. Enter tempo into BPM.

4. Have fun practicing!

Modifications:
 If the number of beats is not 4, you can enter how many beats is in 1 measure into Beat. This changes the number of dots to reflect the number of beats per measure.
If you want a countdown, check the timer checkbox and enter the number of seconds you want.
If you don't know the specific BPM number, you can press the Tap button at the BPM you desire and it will calculate your BPM.
